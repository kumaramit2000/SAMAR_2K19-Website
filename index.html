ABOUT SAMAR'19

The Annual Sports Fest of NIT Raipur

SAMAR is the Annual College Sports Festival of NIT Raipur being organised by "SHAURYA" The Sports Committee of NIT Raipur. It is a four day event held in the month of January or February in which students from various colleges across Chhattisgarh come forward to participate into. Over the years SAMAR has evolved and expanded to become the best sports extravanza. One experiences the 4 days of awesomeness here. Being the most awaited event of the year, it is a platform where youths get together and showcase their talents and sportsmanship. So come, join and rejoice the awesomeness at Samar!! Nitrr sports boasts of a rich culture,thriving immense participation. Samar was one of the best experience at nitrr and has a rich string of memories linked with it. Samar brings with it a great opportunity to show your endeavour along with fun and enjoyment.

As continuing from the last 10 years, SAMAR 2K19 will accommodate students, rather sportspersons from all over Chhattisgarh. In Toto, so many games, both indoor and outdoor, are going to be organized, chess, badminton, volleyball to name some. Every match will be accompanied by a huge audience to encourage the players. It is going to be a plethora of zest and enthusiasm. Awards and prizes are eagerly waiting to be handed over to the deserved players. So tie your shoe laces and put on your sweat bands. NIT Raipur invites you to participate in this grand fest and enjoy the thrill and titillation in sports in a way done never before.

DIVYA- Social initiative

Every year SHAURYA THE SPORTS COMMITTEE of NIT Raipur organizes SAMAR, Central India's Largest Sports Fest at NIT Raipur to unveil the potential of its sportsmen as well as to gather interest of students in the field of sports.
Team Shaurya firmly believes that apart from keeping a person physically fit, Sports play a major role in building character of a person and it cultivates an unrelenting feeling of tencatiy to never give up.
Team Shaurya also recognises the challenges faced by Specially Abled (Divyang) Children, therefore this year the THEME of SAMAR is Divya - Empowerment of Specially Abled (Divyang) Children, to support them. Under this theme, many sports activities like Wheel Chair Cricket, Wheel Chair Basketball etc. are going to be conducted. For the students not having the capability to speak and listen, games like Badminton, Table Tennis, Chess and Carrom will be organized. Sports Kit will be given to every participant and apart from these sports activities, various cultural activities like Painting, Dancing and Singing will be set up for the participants. This year, Chitrasen Sahu, an International mountain climber, will be a guest speaker in the Motivational Talk Session, going to be organized in SAMAR’19.
Along with these events, various Technical Workshops will be organized under the guidance of Divyang Entrepreneurs from all over India, to make them familiar with the latest technologies beneficial for them.
Each Particpant will be awarded with a Jersey at the end of the Programme. After all the events and workshops, the participants will be assessed by the team for six months to notice the impact of sports in their lives..
