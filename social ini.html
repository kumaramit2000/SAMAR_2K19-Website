Social Initiatives

Help Shaurya, in making the Society to be a better place..

UDDAN

Every year SHAURYA organizes this sports fest to encourages the students towards fitness. Apart from promoting psychomotor skills it teaches art of winning, losing and teamwork. It includes inter-branch as well as inter-college competitions. This is the most awaited fest of our college as it includes mass participation of students and provide every student chance to show their talent.

DIVYA

Every year SHAURYA THE SPORTS COMMITTEE of NIT Raipur organizes SAMAR, Central India's Largest Sports Fest at NIT Raipur to unveil the potential of its sportsmen as well as to gather interest of students in the field of sports.
Team Shaurya firmly believes that apart from keeping a person physically fit, Sports play a major role in building character of a person and it cultivates an unrelenting feeling of tencatiy to never give up. Team Shaurya also recognises the challenges faced by Specially Abled (DIvyang) Children, therefore this year the THEME of SAMAR is Divya - Empowerment of Specially Abled (Divyang) Children, to support them. Under this theme, many sports activities like Wheel Chair Cricket, Wheel Chair Basketball etc. are going to be conducted. For the students not having the capability to speak and listen, games like Badminton, Table Tennis, Chess and Carrom will be organized. Sports Kit will be given to every participant and apart from these sports activities, various cultural activities like Painting, Dancing and Singing will be set up for the participants. This year, Chitrasen Sahu, an International Paralympic Player, will be a guest speaker in the Motivational Talk Session, going to be organized in SAMAR’19.

UTTHAN

Every year SHAURYA organizes this sports fest to encourages the students towards fitness. Utthan, as the name suggests, refers to 'upliftment'. We as a group of enthusiastic and determined youth wish to work in his direction for shaping a better future and in ensuring a better world. Come and join us as we embark on this beautiful journey to bring about a change in our society by educating these young minds.
